What you can do with mindLAMP

From data collection to actionable insights, mindLAMP covers every step. Explore each capability to see real-world examples and how-to guides.

Engage Participants

LAMP stands for Learn, Assess, Manage, and Prevent. The app gives participants a place to build skills, complete assessments, and practice wellness activities — while each project configures exactly what that looks like for their population.

Why teams choose mindLAMP

Truly Modular

Configure activities, schedules, groups, app layout, and sensor collection without writing code.

Consistent Data Format

Every project produces data in the same structure, making it easy to compare across sites and populations.

Real-Time Quality Monitoring

Catch missing data before it becomes a problem. Dashboards flag participants who need outreach.

Open Source

Inspect, modify, and extend the code. Adapt the platform as your needs evolve.
