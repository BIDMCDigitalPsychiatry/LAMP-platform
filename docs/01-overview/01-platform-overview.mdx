# About mindLAMP

mindLAMP is an open-source platform for digital phenotyping, clinical research, and remote patient monitoring. It combines a participant-facing mobile app, a web-based coordinator dashboard, background sensor data collection, and an analytics pipeline into a single integrated system.

mindLAMP is developed and maintained by the Division of Digital Psychiatry at Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center (BIDMC), a Harvard Medical School teaching hospital.
