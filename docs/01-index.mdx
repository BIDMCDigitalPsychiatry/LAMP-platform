{/* The Challenge               */}
{/* =========================== */}

<section className="container margin-vert--lg">
  <h2>The Challenge</h2>

  <div className="row">
    <div className="col col--6">
      <h3>Measurement</h3>
      <p>
        Conditions affecting the brain and behavior — including mental health,
        cognition, and neurological disorders — are often assessed through self-report
        questionnaires, interviews, and clinician observations. These methods, while
        valuable, can be inconsistent, infrequent, and influenced by recall or response
        bias. Symptoms frequently fluctuate daily or even hourly, yet traditional clinic
        visits capture only isolated “snapshots” of experience. Critical patterns and
        warning signs can go undetected.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div className="col col--6">
      <h3>Access to Care</h3>
      <p>
        Access to care is constrained by multiple systemic barriers: provider
        shortages and high demand create long wait times; high costs and lack of
        insurance coverage limit access; stigma discourages many from seeking support;
        and geographic barriers — especially in rural or underserved areas — make
        in-person treatment difficult. These challenges leave many without the care or
        data they need.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

{/* =========================== */}
{/* The Solution                */}
{/* =========================== */}

<section className="container margin-vert--lg">
  <h2>The Solution: mindLAMP</h2>
  <p>
    mindLAMP is an open‑source digital health platform designed to support a wide range
    of use cases in psychiatry, psychology, neurology, and cognitive science. It
    addresses challenges of both <strong>measurement</strong> and <strong>access to care</strong>.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Better Measurement:</strong> Capture real‑time, high‑resolution insights through smartphone sensors, surveys, and cognitive tasks.</li>
    <li><strong>Improved Access:</strong> Provide support, psychoeducation, and monitoring directly through a smartphone.</li>
    <li><strong>Research–Clinical Synergy:</strong> Already deployed in clinics, ensuring innovations translate directly into patient care.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    Today, mindLAMP is listed in the <strong>World Health Organization Digital Health Atlas</strong>, used worldwide by research groups, and implemented in busy community clinics, including <strong>Harvard Medical School teaching hospitals</strong>.
  </p>
</section>

{/* =========================== */}
{/* Key Features                */}
{/* =========================== */}

<section className="container margin-vert--lg">
  <h2>Key Features</h2>

  <div className="row">
    <div className="col col--4">
      <h3>Measurement Tools</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>
          <strong>Digital Phenotyping:</strong> Collect granular data about behaviors and
          symptoms via smartphone and connected‑device sensors across operating systems.
          The <strong>Cortex</strong> pipeline helps teams (1) assess data quality in real time,
          (2) derive replicable clinical features (e.g., mobility, sleep), and
          (3) generate easy‑to‑share visualizations. Example: computing
          <em> hometime</em> from thousands of GPS points.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Ecological Momentary Assessment (EMA):</strong> Monitor behaviors and symptoms
          in real time, including metadata about app engagement.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Customizable Surveys:</strong> Configure text, icons, schedules, and
          notifications to capture the data you need.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Cognition:</strong> A suite of validated cognitive tasks studied across mental
          health (e.g., depression, schizophrenia) and neurological conditions (e.g.,
          Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s), supporting research and clinical applications.
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>

    <div className="col col--4">
      <h3>Clinical &amp; Research Support</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>
          <strong>Dashboard:</strong> Design activities, deliver surveys, visualize near‑real‑time
          data, and explore comprehensive datasets via the <strong>Data Portal</strong>.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Flexible Protocols:</strong> Run common study designs and add site‑specific
          questions or schedules as needed.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Distributed Deployment:</strong> Store data centrally or locally to comply with
          local, state, federal, and international laws; optionally deploy from a central
          site for teams not wishing to manage infrastructure.
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>

    <div className="col col--4">
      <h3>Patient Engagement</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>
          <strong>Mobile App (iOS &amp; Android):</strong> Surveys, cognitive games, meditation,
          breathing exercises, psychoeducation, and stress‑management resources.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Optional Passive Sensing:</strong> Accelerometer, GPS, pedometer, and more —
          running unobtrusively in the background when enabled.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Care Team Connection:</strong> Optional in‑app messaging and clinician dashboards
          to connect patients and care teams.
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Flexible Hosting:</strong> Secure upload to servers owned by your organization
          (or hosted by CORE). All content and sensors can be fully customized and
          toggled on/off to fit use cases ranging from medication reminders to full
          digital‑phenotyping suites.
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

  <div className="row">
    <div className="col col--6">
      <h3>③ Database</h3>
      <p>
        A secure, chronological index of all data built on the <strong>LAMP Protocol</strong>.
        It enables reproducible, shareable science and real‑time triggers for custom
        analyses. Host locally or have CORE support a private, secure deployment.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div className="col col--6">
      <h3>④ Cortex</h3>
      <p>
        An open‑source pipeline that connects to the database to derive clinically
        meaningful features (e.g., sedentary behavior, hometime, device usage, sleep
        estimates), produce interactive visualizations, and support just‑in‑time
        adaptive interventions. Run default or custom analyses per your needs.
      </p>
    </div>
