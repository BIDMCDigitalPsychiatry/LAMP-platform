# The mindLAMP App

> For a high-level overview of the participant experience, see [Engage Participants](/capabilities/experience).

The mindLAMP app is the primary interface for participants in research studies and clinical programs. It is available on iOS, Android, and as a web application at [dashboard.lamp.digital](https://dashboard.lamp.digital).

Through the app, participants complete activities like surveys, cognitive games, and self-management exercises, while background sensors passively collect data such as GPS, accelerometer, and screen state. The app also delivers educational content, notifications, and visualizations of the participant's own data.
